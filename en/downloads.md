## Before You Download

- This repository fully drops the old VB.NET frontend path and now centers on the Avalonia desktop frontend.
- Windows compatibility is still being improved and may still have rough edges.
- If you need the safest Windows-first experience today, PCL or PCL-CE remains the safer recommendation.
